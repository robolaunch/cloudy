
 ## Hardware Setup

This page details the hardware setup instructions for Cloudy.

## Tools Needed

 - Philips screwdriver
    For Screwing screws
 - Pliers
    For Removing supports
 - Circlip pliers
    for securing circlips
 - Assembly hammer
    To tightly fit bearings

## Step 3: Caster wheels, bearings

 Fit skf-634 bearings in the pockets as shown in the picture below.

 Lightly tap on outer rings of the bearings. bearings should fit tightly.

 Use circlip pliers and circlips to secure bearings in their places, note that some bearings dont have circlip grooves those bearings will be secured when assembled to the frame 


You will need;
- Hammer for bearing assembly
- circlip pliers 

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/robolaunch/cloudy_stl/main/images/CASTERWHEELS%20BEARINGS.png" width="800" height="430" align="top">

## Step 6: Drive wheels, nuts

 Fit M3 and M4 hex nuts as shown in the picture below. Lightly tap or use pliers to fit nuts if necessary. Take caution not to damage parts. 
  
You will need;
- pliers for inserting nuts
- Hammer for tapping nuts

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/robolaunch/cloudy_stl/main/images/DRIVEWHEELNUTS.png" width="800" height="400" align="top">

## Step 7: Drive wheels, tensioner
 Slide motor flange in to beltbox base part. This step should be done before motor assembly. Parts should fit tightly. Take caution not damaging the parts. 

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/robolaunch/cloudy_stl/main/images/DR%C4%B0VEWHEEL%20TENSIONER.png" width="800" height="410" align="top">

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Step 8: Drive wheels, motor

Assemble motor, motor pulley and 50T pulley with bearings.
Do not tighten belt tensioning screws(M3X25) for easily assembling belt.
Motor connection pins should look up.

You will need;
- Hammer
- screwdriver

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/robolaunch/cloudy_stl/main/images/DRIVEWHEELSSEMIASSEM.png" width="800" height="560" align="top">

You should end up with two semi assembled components shown in the picture above.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Step 9: Drive wheels, belt

Assemble belt, then beltbox right and left,

Tighten belt using tightening screws equally after assembling beltbox cap. Do not overtighten belts. belts should have a play about 1mms.

You will need a screwdriver

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/robolaunch/cloudy_stl/main/images/DRIVEWHEELSFIN.png" width="700" height="900" rotate="90" align="top">

You should end up with two semi assembled components shown in the picture above.
